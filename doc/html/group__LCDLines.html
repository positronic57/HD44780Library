#define LCD16x1_SELECT_DDRAM_1ST_HALF   0x80
DDRAM of single line LCD in most of the cases is divided in two 8 bytes wide memory segments. The starting addresses of those segments are defined as:
  • LCD16x1_SELECT_DDRAM_1ST_HALF
  • LCD16x1_SELECT_DDRAM_2ND_HALF Those LCD modules although they have a single line, due to the DDRAM layout must be configured as two line modules.
